    PRI (Pesta Rakyat Indonesia) is an annual festival celebrating Indonesian art and culture, nurturing talent and creativity among students. PRI 2023's theme is "PANCARONA DWIPANTARA," uniting modern and traditional cultures. It showcases Indonesia's cultural evolution through student and guest performances, while also educating and introducing Indonesian and international students to Indonesia's rich and diverse culture with an emphasis on preservation.
